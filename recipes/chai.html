Chai

Description

Hot milk tea, good for a tired day.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of water.
  • 1 cup of milk.
  • 1 tbsp of tea leaves.
  • 2 tbsp of sugar.

Steps

  1. Heat the water in a pot.
  2. Add tea leaves to the pot when the water starts to boil.
  3. Add milk and sugar.
  4. Pour into a cup.
  5. Chai is ready :)
