Maggi

Description

Instant noodles, served with veggies.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of Maggi.
  • 1 cup of water.
  • Chopped and boiled vegetables.

Steps

  1. Heat the water in a pot.
  2. Add the maggi and the tastemaker powder to the pot when the water starts to boil.
  3. Add the veggies.
  4. Stir for 2 minutes.
  5. Maggi is ready :)
